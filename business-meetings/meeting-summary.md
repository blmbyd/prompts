# Meeting Summary

Summarize the meeting into a structured and actionable report.

## Output Requirements

-   **Meeting Header:** Include Title, Date, Time, and Attendees.
-   **Executive Summary:** A brief 2-3 sentence overview of the meeting.
-   **Key Decisions:** A bulleted list of all decisions made.
-   **Action Items:** A table with columns for Task, Owner, and Deadline for each action item.
-   **Next Steps:** Any follow-up activities or the next meeting's date/time.

Please ensure the summary is clear and concise.
