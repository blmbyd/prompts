# Emails

Prompts for email drafting and response generation.

## Prompts

### Professional Email Draft

```
Draft a professional email based on the provided context and requirements.

OUTPUT REQUIREMENTS:
-   **Subject Line:** A clear and concise subject line.
-   **Greeting:** An appropriate professional greeting.
-   **Body:** Well-structured email content with clear paragraphs.
-   **Call to Action:** A clear next step or request if applicable.
-   **Closing:** A professional sign-off.

Please ensure the tone is professional and the message is concise.
```
